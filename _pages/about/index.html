Hello! My name is Yongjia Deng (邓永嘉, she/her). I am a second-year PhD student from the Language and Science Program at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, working with Professor Jacee Cho. I completed my B.A. in Linguistics with distinction at Purdue University in 2024. Having grown up as a Hakka heritage speaker in southeastern China, I can speak Mandarin Chinese, Hakka, and a little Cantonese.

My research interests lie in are psycholinguistics and Second Language Acquisition(SLA).
